Dankium Browser

Your Web, Your Way. Focused on Privacy, Powered by Chromium.

Experience a Cleaner, More Private Web

Dankium is a personal project dedicated to providing you with a fast, secure, and respectful **Browse** experience. Built on the solid foundation of Chromium, Dankium integrates powerful content filtering technology from eyeo GmbH (the creators of Adblock Plus), giving you control over what you see online.

Forget intrusive ads and unnecessary distractions. With Dankium, your focus returns to the content that truly matters to you.

Privacy-First Design

Your **Browse** data stays on your device. We do not collect your history, bookmarks, or personal **Browse** activity on our servers.

Built on Chromium

Enjoy the speed, security, and familiarity of the Chromium engine, constantly updated for optimal performance.

Integrated Content Filtering

Leverage eyeo's advanced technology to block unwanted ads and pop-ups for a smoother, cleaner web.

Enhanced Security with Memory Tagging

Dankium supports memory tagging, a cutting-edge security feature that helps prevent memory corruption vulnerabilities, significantly reducing potential exploits.

Robust Compiler Mitigations

Our browser incorporates advanced compiler mitigations, adding layers of defense against various types of attacks and enhancing overall system integrity.

JavaScript Interpretation Only

To further enhance security, Dankium interprets JavaScript rather than compiling it, substantially reducing the attack surface for a wide range of exploits.
